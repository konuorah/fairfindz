Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

Overview

FairFindz is a Chrome extension that displays alternative products while you browse supported pages. This policy describes what information the extension processes and why.

Information the Extension Processes

Amazon page context

When you are on an Amazon product page, the extension may read page content needed to understand the product (for example, title, price text, and product details) so it can show relevant alternatives.

Extension settings

The extension stores configuration settings in Chrome storage (for example, your Supabase project configuration if you set it in the Options page). This data stays within your browser profile.

Authentication/session state (if enabled)

If the extension offers sign-in or guest session functionality, it may store session-related tokens or flags locally in Chrome storage to keep you signed in or maintain preferences.

What We Collect

We do not sell personal information. We do not collect sensitive personal information.

How We Use Information

  • Show alternative products relevant to the Amazon product page you are viewing.
  • Operate extension features you enable, such as configuration and session persistence.
  • Troubleshoot and improve reliability (for example, basic error logs in the browser console).

Data Sharing

The extension may communicate with your configured Supabase backend to retrieve product data. We do not share your personal information with third parties for advertising.

Data Storage and Retention

Settings and session-related data are stored locally using Chrome storage and remain until you clear extension data or uninstall the extension.
